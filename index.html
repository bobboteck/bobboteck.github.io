Followino

Followino is the result of past experiences on the idea of "Line Follower for Everyone": we thought of something that was really easy to accomplish and had basic middle level performance but at the same time could give a margin of personalization and improvement in the performance. Followino aims to be a suitable educational solution for any age, based on Arduino as a control system, and as mentioned above offers the possibility of being easily customized: it is a solution that can allow everyone to approach the Robotics and Programming, with the stimulus of "competition" as an incentive for the study. Those who come close to this project offer not only the possibility of mechanical and aesthetic customization, but also the ability to think about how to use some expansion ports (including a Bluetooth) with which to create an App to handle the parameters of configuration or direct control of the Robot. To accompany the Education side of the project, handouts and other resources that will explain how it works and how to make your own Robot LineFollower Followino.
